# SmartClause 2.0 – Comprehensive User Guide

Welcome to **SmartClause**, your professional AI-powered legal drafting and matter management assistant. This guide will help you master the platform's features to streamline your legal workflow.

---

## 🚀 1. Getting Started

### Access & Logic
- **Login/Signup**: Use your professional email to create an account.
- **Persistent Sessions**: Your login survives browser refreshes and page navigation.
- **Sidebar Navigation**:
    - **Matters**: Your command center for all legal files.
    - **Clause Library**: Reusable legal precedents.
    - **Organization**: Manage your firm and team members.
    - **Pricing**: Manage your subscription and credits.

---

## 📂 2. Matter Management

Matters are the folders where you organize documents and case details for a specific client or legal issue.

### Creating a Matter
1. Click the **[+ New Matter]** button in the sidebar.
2. Enter the Matter Name, Client, and Jurisdiction (Kenya by default).
3. Choose a Matter Type (e.g., Contract, Litigation).

### Managing Matters
- **Filter**: Use the **Filter** button in the Matters list to find items by status (Active/Review), type, or jurisdiction.
- **Pinning**: Pin your most active matters to the top of your list for quick access.
- **Archiving**: Move completed matters to "Archived" to keep your workspace clean.

---

## ⚖️ 3. Case Manager

Deep inside every matter, the **Case Manager** helps you track the procedural side of your files.

- **Timeline (Events)**: Log court dates, meetings, and filings.
- **Tasks**: Create to-do items with priority levels (Urgent, High, Medium, Low).
- **Notes**: Keep professional internal reminders about the matter progress.

---

## ✍️ 4. AI-Powered Document Drafting

### Generating a New Document
1. From a Matter page, click **[+ New Document]**.
2. Select a template type (e.g., NDA, Employment Contract).
3. Fill in the "Key Terms" form. The AI uses these variables to draft the initial document.

### The Document Editor
- **AI Chat Assistant**: Use the sidebar chat to request changes. You can say *"Make the termination clause 30 days"* or *"Add a non-compete section."*
- **Version Control**: 
    - **Save Version**: Creates a minor save point for your work.
    - **Major Versions**: Use this for stable drafts you want to keep permanently.
- **Exporting**: Click **[Export to DOCX]** to download a professionally formatted Microsoft Word file.

---

## 📚 5. Clause Library

Stop hunting through old folders for that "one perfect clause."

- **The Vault**: Access a library of system-provided legal clauses.
- **Custom Clauses**: Add your own "Boilerplate," "Warranties," or "Termination" language.
- **Quick Copy**: Use the **[⋮]** menu to copy a clause to your clipboard and paste it directly into your drafting window.

## 👥 6. Organization & Billing

### Team Management

## 🛡️ 7. Security & Compliance

- **Kenyan Data Protection**: SmartClause is built to comply with the **Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019**.
- **Data Privacy**: Your documents are secured with Row-Level Security, meaning only authorized members of your organization can access them.
- **Not Legal Advice**: Remember, SmartClause is a productivity tool. All AI-generated content should be reviewed by a qualified legal professional.
